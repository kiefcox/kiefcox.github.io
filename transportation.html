Getting around the island.

The easiest way to travel is by bus

The public bus system in Taniti city is great and can get you wherever in the city you would like to go. Public busses run from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM every day

Going outside of Taniti city? That's ok! We have private busses that access all other parts if the Island.

Want more control over where you are going?

Taxis are available in the city and can get you wherever you need to go and quickly.

Rental cars are also available, giving you maximum freedom to explore the islan exaxtly how you want.

Taniti is very easy no matter your mode of transportation!

Whether you are walking or biking, Taniti is easy to navegate

Rent a bike for your stay and get everywhere you want in this beautiful and fairly flat city, just be sure you are wearing a helmet!

Since Taniti city is so flat, it's extremely walkable. Most visitors stay in the area arround Merriton Landing. This area is so easy to explore on foot you'll never want a car!
