Here are some frequently asked questions about the island

What are the power outlets like?

Power outlets are 120 volts, which is the same as the United States

Are there any alcohol regulations?

Yes! Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 AM

What is the legal drinking age on Taniti?

That would be 18 years old

More questions? Ask away!

I don't speak Tanitian... will I be OK?

Yes, of course. Many younger Tanitians actually speak fluent english. However, in rural areas it may be harder to find an english speaker, especially among the elderly.

What if I need medical attention?

There is one hospital and several clinics on the island. You will be able to recieve the care that you need.

How safe is Taniti?

Violent crime is almost non-existent. However, with the rise in tourism there have been some reports of pickpocketing and other petty theft

Do I need to change my currency?

No, Taniti uses the US dollar as it's currency

Are there holidays?

Yes, there are a large number of national holidays where attractions and restaurants will be closed. Tourists should plan accordingly

If you ever have more questions, feel free to ask a Tanitian!
